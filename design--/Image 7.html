National ID Card Renewal (CIN)

Renewal process for Tunisian national identity cards due to expiration, damage, or data changes. Ensuring administrative continuity for citizens.

timeline

Step-by-Step Procedure

1

Document Preparation

Gather all required documents mentioned in the checklist above. Ensure photographs meet the national biometric standards.

2

Visit Your Local Police District

Head to the police station corresponding to your official place of residence. You do not need an appointment, but morning hours are recommended.

3

Filing and Biometrics

Submit your file to the officer. They will verify documents and may capture a digital thumbprint if your previous data is outdated.

4

Collection

A receipt is provided with a pickup date (usually 10-15 business days). Present the receipt and your old CIN to collect the new card.
